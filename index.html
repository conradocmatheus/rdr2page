Synopsis

Red Dead Redemption 2 is a prequel that tells the story of Arthur Morgan, a member of the Van der Linde gang, as they navigate the rugged landscapes of America's heartland in 1899. Fleeing from the law after a failed robbery, the gang struggles to survive while grappling with their own internal conflicts and dwindling opportunities in a changing world. As Arthur, players witness his journey of loyalty, redemption, and self-discovery, making choices that shape his fate and the fate of those around him in this epic tale set against the backdrop of the fading Wild West.
